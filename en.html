Island of Rab

The island of Rab is located on the northern part of the Adriatic Sea as part of the Kvarner archipelago. Beside the town of Rab, there are 7 more places on the island: Lopar, Barbat, Banjol, Palit, Kampor, Mundanije and Supetarska Draga. There are approximately 9,000 inhabitants living on Rab. Organised tourism if flourishing for more than 125 years, as well as accompanying activities (fishing, livestock breeding, olive growing, wine industry, shipbuilding...). The development of tourism on the island is primarily possible because of a pleasant Mediterranean climate whose main feature are warm summers and mild winters...   More about the island
